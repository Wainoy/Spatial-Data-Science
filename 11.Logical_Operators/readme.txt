AND - statements are executed iff both conditions evaluate to True
OR - statements are executed if at least one condition evaluates to True
NOT - statements are executed if the specified condition is not true.

Sample exercise:
The price of a house is $1M. If the buyer has good credit, they’re required to put down 10%. If they have bad credit, they’re required to put down 20%. Write a program with these conditions and print the amount a good buyer ought to pay.
